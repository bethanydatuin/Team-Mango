West Tennessee Fil-Am Association

Welcome

Mabuhay, and welcome to the West Tennessee Fil-Am Association website! Located in Jackson, Tennessee, our goal is to promote camaraderie and understanding among our Filipino-American friends in West Tennessee by preserving and enriching our diverse and rich cultural heritage through various social, civic, and charitable activities.

This website will serve as a communication and information dissemination vehicle within our membership. Please feel free to browse around and explore this website as it will give you a lot of information about our organization's history, activities, and photo gallery showing our different activities. If you haven't joined our group, you can easily download our membership registration form. Of course, you are welcome to send us an email anytime if you have any questions, comments, or suggestions. Don't hesitate to contact us. We hope you'll enjoy your stay here.

Maraming salamat po!
